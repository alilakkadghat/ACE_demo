🌱 Why Ayuva?

Currently, Ayurvedic diet charts are still written manually. Existing software focuses on macro/micro nutrients but ignores Ayurvedic concepts like Rasa (six tastes) and food properties (hot/cold, digestibility). Ayuva bridges this gap, offering a modern, intuitive, and Ayurveda-compliant solution.
