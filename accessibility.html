Accessibility

We are committed to making our platform accessible to all users. Below are the measures we have taken to ensure compliance and a better user experience.

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)

Our platform adheres to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards to ensure a wide range of accessibility for people with disabilities.

Available Accommodations

  • Screen reader compatibility for users with visual impairments.
  • Keyboard navigation for those who cannot use a mouse.
  • High contrast mode and adjustable font sizes for better readability.
